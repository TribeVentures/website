Overview

Overview

A fast-paced concept pitch-competition in front of W&M students and a panel of judges hosted every Wednesday in the Entrepreneurship Center. Prize awarded to best pitch!

Purpose

To equip W&M students with entrepreneurial ideas by identifying problems, initiating potential solutions, validating the solutions by testing the market and pivoting for better ones.

Why

Quick concept pitches challenge entrepreneurs to refine their ideas into the most important details while getting rid of distractions. This is how startups are born!
